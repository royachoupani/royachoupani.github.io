I am an associate professor of Computer Science at [Angelo State University](http://www.angelo.edu/computer-science)'s Department of Computer Science. I recevied my PhD in Computer Science from [TU Delft](https://www.tudelft.nl/en/) in Netherlands. I have worked with Professor [Stephan Wong](https://www.tudelft.nl/eemcs/the-faculty/departments/quantum-computer-engineering/computer-engineering/staff/stephan-wong/) and Professor [Koen Bertels](https://www.tudelft.nl/eemcs/the-faculty/departments/quantum-computer-engineering/quantum-computer-architecture-lab/staff/koen-bertels/). My research and teaching insterests are in:

- Multimedia Computing
- Machine Learning
- Artificial Intelligence
- Database Systems
- Cybersecurity
